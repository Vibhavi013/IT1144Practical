We take great pleasure in welcoming you to the University of Vavuniya! Situated in the land that was once part of the kingdom of the legendary Vanni Kings, the University of Vavuniya is a higher seat of learning and research in this region, which provides higher education in the fields of Applied Science ,Business Management and Technological Studies. As a service provider in the higher education sector we are customer focused – where producing totally satisfied customers is the prime aim of all our endeavours. ‘Customers’ in our perspectives are our (past, present and future) students
